परिचय (About Me)

Welcome to my personal portfolio. I am a proud retiree of the Indian Railways, where I served as the Director of Official Language (राजभाषा). Over my career, I dedicated myself to the promotion, implementation, and enrichment of Hindi within the administrative framework of the world's largest railway network.

Career Highlights & Journey

  • Leadership: Guided the implementation of Rajbhasha policies across various railway zones.
  • Literary Contributions: Supervised the translation, publication, and documentation of official railway manuals and journals.
  • Recognition: Organized nationwide workshops, seminars, and award ceremonies to encourage the usage of Hindi among railway personnel.
